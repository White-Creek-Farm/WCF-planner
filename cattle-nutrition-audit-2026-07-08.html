Data Source Map

1. Feed logs

Source: cattle_dailys.feeds. Quantities: lbs_as_fed, feed name, herd, feed date, nutrition_snapshot, is_creep.

2. Nutrition values

Source: feed-line nutrition_snapshot. Moisture% converts as-fed pounds to DM. CP% and NFC% are then applied to DM. landed_per_lb would drive cost, but it is missing on these lines.

3. Cow units

Source: cattle plus latest cattle weigh-ins. Weight is averaged across the window and divided by 1,000 lb.

4. Targets

Source: cattle_nutrition_targets. Mommas: 2.5% DM, 10% CP, 30% NFC. Finishers: 2.8% DM, 12% CP, 50% NFC.

5. Cards

Output: total feed, cost, DM/day, CP/day, NFC/day, cow units, mortality, and report days for 30/90/120 day windows.

Formula Notes For Audit

Total feed

Total of lbs_as_fed from feed lines inside the selected window.

DM lb/day

Corrected calculation: included lbs_as_fed multiplied by the dry-matter fraction from moisture_pct, then divided by window days.

CP / NFC lb/day

Sum of true dry matter multiplied by nutrition_snapshot protein_pct or nfc_pct, then divided by window days.

Momma creep rule

is_creep feed lines count in total feed and cost, but are excluded from DM, CP, and NFC. No source rows in this audit were flagged as creep.

Cow units

Latest cattle weigh-in per tag, prorated across the window by purchase date. Pounds are divided by 1,000.

Missing cost

Feed cost is unavailable because the feed snapshots in these windows do not carry landed_per_lb values.
